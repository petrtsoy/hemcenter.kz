LLP “Center of Hematology” has been recognized as accredited under Category I for the years 2025–2028 by the Institute for Quality and Accreditation in Healthcare (IQAН).

===

The accreditation confirms that the Center’s activities comply with the national standards of quality and safety of medical care of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
