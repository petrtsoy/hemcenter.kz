Hematology Center, the Public Association “Kazakhstan Society of Hematologists”, and Karaganda Medical University invite you to take part in a series of educational events.

===

Starting September 19, Karaganda Medical University will host a distance continuing education course “Obstetric Hematology” (2 weeks, 120 hours). The course is open to hematologists, general practitioners, obstetrician-gynecologists, and internists.

The program will include lectures on the physiology and pathology of hemostasis in pregnant women, delivered by Professor Tatyana Vladimirovna Vavilova, MD, PhD, Head of the Department of Clinical Laboratory Diagnostics and Genetics at the Almazov National Medical Research Centre in St. Petersburg. Together with Professor Vavilova, clinical case studies from practice will also be discussed. You are welcome to send your cases in advance within the course theme to our email: clinical_case@hemcenter.kz
.

Participants of the educational cycle will have the opportunity to improve their theoretical knowledge and professional skills in managing pregnant women with blood disorders.
Course fee: 42,200 KZT (VAT included).

Applications for participation can be submitted via email: zharbolova@qmu.kz
, ramazanova@qmu.kz
.
