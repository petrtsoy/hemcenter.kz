Center was visited by Natalia Vladimirovna Makarevich, an expert in pathomorphology from Pavlov First Saint Petersburg State Medical University.

===

As a senior laboratory assistant of the Research and Clinical Center of Pathomorphology of Pavlov First Saint Petersburg State Medical University, she spent several days conducting seminars, practical sessions, and master classes for the staff of LLP Hematology Center, Karaganda branch. She introduced new working methods and, together with the department staff, reviewed pressing issues related to technological processes.

Natalia Vladimirovna gave a positive assessment of the department’s work, including its approach to developing standard operating procedures, and noted the competence and professionalism of both physicians and staff. She was particularly satisfied with the performance of laboratory technicians and their high level of expertise.

She was also impressed by her visit to the laboratory of the Department of Pathological Anatomy at Karaganda Medical University, one of the Hematology Center’s partners, and described this cooperation as promising.
