Alyona Vasilievna Zinchenko, Deputy Director for Clinical Affairs of the Karaganda Branch of LLP “Center of Hematology,” has been awarded the honorary badge “Excellence in Healthcare” within the framework of the national competition “Excellence in Healthcare – 2025.”

===

Alyona Vasilievna is a highly skilled physician and a true leader of the clinical team. She possesses deep expertise in hematological diseases, is an outstanding diagnostician, works with the most complex categories of patients, strives to select the most effective treatment for each individual, and fights for every patient’s life without fear of challenges.

As a mentor, Alyona Vasilievna trains and supports young specialists, fostering a culture of responsibility, commitment to development, and professional growth.

We sincerely congratulate Alyona Vasilievna on this well-deserved award and express our gratitude for her dedication to the profession and to her patients!
