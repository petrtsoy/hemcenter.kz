The seasonal Hematology Schools are organized by the Public Foundation “Kazakhstan Professional Society of Oncohematologists.” These Schools bring together doctors from all over Kazakhstan to exchange experience, analyze clinical cases, and train residents and young hematologists in properly presenting clinical information.

===

Staff of the Hematology Center have repeatedly taken part in these Schools as speakers. This time, however, 12 representatives of the Center delivered presentations.

Among the speakers and experts were Irina A. Pivovarova, General Director of LLP “Hematology Center,” and Anton A. Klodzinsky, Medical Director; as well as Meruert S. Sataeva, Liliya D. Zablotskaya, Zhazira K. Serikbolova, Ayauzhan E. Yesimbekova, Andrey V. Zinchenko, Natalia E. Antonova, Marzhan A. Kahanova, Akmaral Zh. Anafina, Malika S. Saulepekova, and Anastasiya O. Lysenko. Participants of the School highlighted the high level of preparation and professionalism of our speakers.

The Hematology Center expresses its gratitude to the Kazakhstan Professional Society of Oncohematologists for organizing the School and for many years of partnership.
