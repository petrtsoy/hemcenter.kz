In July, General Director Irina Pivovarova and Medical Director Anton Klodzinsky visited the R. M. Gorbacheva Research Institute of Pediatric Oncology, Hematology and Transplantation, the First St. Petersburg State Medical University named after I. P. Pavlov, the North-Western State Medical University named after I. I. Mechnikov, the V. A. Almazov National Medical Research Center, LabWave LLC, the Russian Research Institute of Hematology and Transfusiology of the Federal Medical-Biological Agency, the City Center for the Treatment of Patients with Hemophilia (St. Petersburg), and UNIM LLC (Moscow).

Discussions focused on opportunities for collaboration in the diagnosis of hematological diseases, including through remote technologies, as well as in education and participation in scientific research.
